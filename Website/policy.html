Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

Welcome to MirrorMe. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MirrorMe ("we," "our," or "us") collects the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name provided during registration.
  • Profile Data: Optional details such as name, age, height, weight, and style preferences used to personalize your experience.
  • Outfit Photos: Photos you upload for AI analysis. These are processed by our AI service to generate style feedback and are stored to maintain your analysis history.
  • Wardrobe Data: Clothing items detected and saved to your digital wardrobe.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, and analysis history to improve the service.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app usage analytics.
  • Subscription Information: Limited transaction metadata related to your Premium subscription (e.g., subscription token, transaction ID, product ID). We do not collect or store raw payment card numbers; these are handled securely by the Apple App Store.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI fashion styling services
  • Personalize your fashion styling experience
  • Generate outfit analysis, style scores, and styling recommendations
  • Build and maintain your digital wardrobe
  • Validate your subscription and maintain access to Premium features
  • Send important updates about your account or our services
  • Analyze app usage to improve functionality

2.1 Subscription Model

MirrorMe is available as a freemium app with two optional Premium plans:

  • Monthly — €9.99/month: Billed monthly, cancel anytime.
  • Annual — €29.99/year: Billed once per year (approx. €2.50/month).

Both plans include up to 100 outfit analyses per month, priority AI processing, and full analysis history access. Free users receive 2 outfit analyses in total.

Subscriptions are processed by the Apple App Store. We only store the minimal subscription token necessary to verify entitlement and prevent fraud. You may manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With Google LLC — outfit photos are transmitted to the Google Gemini API for AI style analysis and stored in Google Firebase Storage. Google processes this data as a data processor on our behalf, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • With Ximilar s.r.o. — outfit photos are transmitted to the Ximilar Fashion Tagging API for clothing detection. Ximilar processes images transiently for classification and does not retain them beyond the API call, subject to Ximilar's Privacy Policy.
  • With service providers who help us operate our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • With app store/platform providers for purchase validation and fraud prevention

In-app disclosure and consent: Before any outfit analysis is performed, the app presents a clear notice identifying the data to be shared (your outfit photo and any profile details you have provided), the specific third-party recipients (Ximilar s.r.o. and Google LLC), and a link to this Privacy Policy. Your analysis will only proceed once you have explicitly agreed to this notice. Both Ximilar s.r.o. and Google LLC are contractually required to provide data protections that are equivalent to those described in this Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Subscription verification tokens may be retained to confirm entitlement and prevent fraud. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (subject to minimal retention required for fraud prevention and accounting).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Data portability (where applicable under GDPR)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

If subscription pricing, data processing practices, or other significant terms change, we will highlight those modifications clearly and, where required, seek consent.
